A new web presence for Digital HPS!

Submitted by Erick Peirson on Thu, 02/07/2013 - 20:16

At the most recent meeting of the Digital HPS Consortium, we decided to develop a new webpage to provide a more accessible clearing-house for information and resources, and to improve dissemination and growth of the dHPS community. To that end a team at Arizona State University Center for Biology and Society, led by PhD student Erica O'Neil, has created a new Drupal-based website at digitalHPS.org.
