Judith Magee

Judith is responsible for the day-to-day mechanics of the Wallace Correspondence Project e.g. managing project staff and the budget.

Judith is Curator of Library Special Collections in the NHM. She has worked for the Museum for about fifteen years and has secured grants and project managed several digitisation projects that have gone online. These include the art collections from Captain Cook’s three voyages and the art collections from the First Fleet.
